Doublesided Thesis in Postscript

The thesis is available as two postscript files suitable for printing in double-sided page format. To print the thesis, first print the odd-numbered pages. Turn this copy over and place the paper back into the printer. Then print the even-numbered pages (they are in reverse order). The result will be easier on the world's paper (forest) supply.
