MIDCA instructions

1) be on metacog/erewhon
2) cd /fs/metacog/group/systems/MIDCA1.01
or cd /fs/erewhon/group/systems/MIDCA1.01
3a) python2.7 midca_inst.py
or
3b) python2.7 /fs/metacog/group/systems/MIDCA_1.02_Michael/midca.py
4) press enter to step through - each enter moves through one phase, e.g. note, sim.
5) q + enter to quit
