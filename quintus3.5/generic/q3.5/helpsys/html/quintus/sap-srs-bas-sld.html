Shared Libraries and Delivering Execuatables

WARNING: An application may depend upon shared libraries, but they are separate from the executable. So it is necessary to take explicit steps to ensure that they will in fact be present when the application is run on the target machine. They must be explicitly linked in to the deliverable as described in sap-rge-sos.
