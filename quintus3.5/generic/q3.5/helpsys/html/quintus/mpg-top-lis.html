List Processing


T =.. L
the functor and arguments of term T comprise the list L
append(A,B,C)
the list C is the concatenation of lists A and B
keysort(L,S)
the list L sorted by key yields S
length(L,N)
the length of list L is N
sort(L,S)
sorting the list L into order yields S
