This section describes displays and screens for ProXL. We also discuss the concepts of displayables and screenables, and the default screen and display.

Both displays and screens have many attributes that can be examined by the get_display_attributes/[1,2] and get_screen_attributes/[1,2] predicates. It is not possible to set any attributes of displays or screens.
